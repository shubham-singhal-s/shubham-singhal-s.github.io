Generative Image Model
Western Sydney University
2023
  • Trained an AI model to produce high-resolution images of mountains and landscapes.
  • Model could generate 256x256 images of mountains and landscapes.
  • Developed a BigGAN model from scratch using Tensorflow and Keras.
  • The model had over 80 million parameters and required multiple high capacity GPUs to train.
  • Training was done on University's high-performance computing cluster that consisted of multiple NVIDIA A100 GPUs.
  • Model was on par with the original BigGAN model in terms of image quality, but was optimised to require significantly less memory.
  • This involved data gathering and cleaning, hyperparameter tuning and training over multiple weeks.
